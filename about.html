About Us

Our Mission

At Anupanas, our mission is to make holistic health accessible. We aim to do so by providing carefully curated workshops, Ayurveda wellness plans, and high-quality self-care products. We strive to improve the quality of your life while building a stronger, healthier, and happier community.

What is Ayurveda

Ayurveda is traditional, plant based medicine originating in India. Although the first written record of Ayurveda was found approximately five thousand (5,000) years ago historians believe that it has been practiced for much longer than that.
Ayurveda focuses on healing a person's mind, body, and soul; not the symptom. This individualized, holistic approach allows Ayurveda to remain one of the most advanced forms of medicine known to man.
